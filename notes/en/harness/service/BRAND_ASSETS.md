## Usage Rules

- Use an icon only to identify or link to content about that service.
- Do not use it as the Today in Tech logo, product icon, or favicon.
- Do not alter its aspect ratio, color, or shape.
- Select the approved color version for the active light or dark theme.
- Display it as a secondary element inside the shared theme surface and clear space.
- Do not imply affiliation, sponsorship, or endorsement.
- Apply updated owner guidelines or removal requests within a reasonable period.
